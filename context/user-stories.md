# User Stories for Stream Scheduling App

## 1. View Stream Schedule
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [zien wanneer mijn favoriete streamer live gaat] **zodat** [ik geen streams mis].
- **Priority**: Must Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - The app displays a list of upcoming streams for followed streamers.
  - The schedule includes the date, time, and platform (e.g., Twitch, YouTube).
  - The schedule is updated in real-time or refreshed periodically.

## 2. Add Stream to Schedule
- **Als** [streamer] **wil ik** [een nieuwe stream toevoegen aan mijn schema] **zodat** [mijn viewers weten wanneer ik live ga].
- **Priority**: Must Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Streamers can add a new stream event with details like date, time, platform, and description.
  - The stream is immediately visible to viewers who follow the streamer.
  - The app validates the input (e.g., no overlapping streams).

## 3. Receive Notifications for Upcoming Streams
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [een melding ontvangen wanneer een streamer live gaat] **zodat** [ik op tijd kan kijken].
- **Priority**: Must Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Viewers can opt-in to receive notifications for specific streamers.
  - Notifications are sent via email, SMS, or app push notification.
  - Notifications are sent 15 minutes before the stream starts.

## 4. Edit or Cancel a Stream
- **Als** [streamer] **wil ik** [een geplande stream bewerken of annuleren] **zodat** [ik mijn viewers op de hoogte kan houden van wijzigingen].
- **Priority**: Must Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Streamers can edit details like date, time, or description of a scheduled stream.
  - Streamers can cancel a stream, and viewers are notified of the cancellation.
  - Changes are immediately reflected in the schedule.

## 5. Filter Streams by Category or Platform
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [streams filteren op categorie of platform] **zodat** [ik gemakkelijk streams kan vinden die mij interesseren].
- **Priority**: Nice to Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Viewers can filter streams by category or platform across all followed streamers.
  - Filtering options include categories (e.g., gaming, music, talk shows) and platforms (e.g., Twitch, YouTube, Facebook Gaming).
  - The filtered results are displayed in real-time.

## 6. Export Schedule to Calendar
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [mijn stream schema exporteren naar mijn kalender] **zodat** [ik het gemakkelijk kan integreren met mijn andere afspraken].
- **Priority**: Nice to Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Viewers can export their stream schedule to Google Calendar, Outlook, or iCal.
  - The exported events include the stream details (e.g., time, platform, description).
  - The export process is seamless and requires minimal user input.

## 7. Follow/Unfollow Streamers
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [streamers kunnen volgen of ontvolgen] **zodat** [ik alleen updates ontvang van streamers die mij interesseren].
- **Priority**: Must Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Viewers can follow or unfollow streamers with a single click.
  - The app updates the viewer's schedule automatically when they follow or unfollow a streamer.
  - The follow status is saved and persists across sessions.

## 8. View Streamer Profile
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [de profielpagina van een streamer kunnen bekijken] **zodat** [ik meer informatie kan vinden over de streamer en hun content].
- **Priority**: Nice to Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Viewers can access a streamer's profile page by clicking on their name.
  - The profile page is publicly accessible to all viewers and includes details like bio, social media links, and past streams.
  - The profile page displays the streamer's upcoming schedule.

## 9. Set Recurring Streams
- **Als** [streamer] **wil ik** [herhalende streams kunnen instellen] **zodat** [ik niet elke keer handmatig een nieuwe stream hoef toe te voegen].
- **Priority**: Nice to Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Streamers can set a stream to repeat daily, weekly, or monthly.
  - The app automatically adds future instances of the stream to the schedule.
  - Streamers can edit or cancel individual instances of a recurring stream.

## 10. View Past Streams
- **Als** [viewer] **wil ik** [eerdere streams van een streamer kunnen bekijken] **zodat** [ik streams kan inhalen die ik heb gemist].
- **Priority**: Nice to Have
- **Definition of Done**:
  - Viewers can access a list of past streams for a specific streamer.
  - The list includes details like date, time, and a link to the stream recording (if available).
  - Past streams are archived and searchable.
